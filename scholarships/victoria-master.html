VICTORIA MASTER (BY THESIS) SCHOLARSHIP

For the purpose of encouraging postgraduate research at Victoria, the University offers scholarships to students about to commence a full-time, research-focussed Master’s degree.

TOTAL VALUE & TENURE

The scholarship will provide:

— Scholarships will be tenable for 1 years. — $15,000 stipend annually + domestic tuition fees. (International students, other than those from Australia will be liable to pay the difference between the full international student fee and the domestic fee)
