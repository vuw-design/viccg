Student presentation at Weta

Some of Victoria's top computer graphics undergraduates visited our colleagues in Miramar to present their work. We were hosted at Park Road Post in June 2017 and Weta Digital in November 2017. The June event saw the final presentations from the fourth-year computer graphics programming project course (CGRA402). The November event showcased the best work from the third-year introduction to computer graphics course for Computer Science majors (COMP308), the fourth-year honours course in rendering (CGRA408) and projects at honours, masters, and Phd level.
