Venue Directions & Map

Workshop 2 is being held at EWHA Womans University in Seoul, Korea

By subway

The Ewha Womans University main gate is located about 10 minutes walk from Ewha Womans University subway station on line number 2 (green line), exit 3.

The back gate is located near Severance Hospital, Yonsei University.

Venue

The workshop is being held at EWHA Womans University in Lecture Hall B102, Science Building B.

Directions from the Main Gate

Enter through the front gate of Ewha Womans University and walk towards Ewha Campus Complex (ECC). Take the way on the left, continue walking about 5 minutes while keeping ECC on your right until you find a four-way intersection. At the intersection, turn left to walk up the slight hill and pass Pharmaceutical science building and Student union on your right and left respectively. Continue to walk up the hill after you pass Ewha-POSCO building. Follow the way on the right and Science building is on the left hand side.
