Courses for Computer Graphics within the School of Engineering and Computer Science and the School of Design

Victoria University offers undergraduate courses in Computer Graphics (in the BSc), Computer Science (in the BSc), and Media Design (in the BDI).

Postgraduate courses, open to graduates of Victoria and other universities, include the BSc(Hons) in Computer Graphics and the MSc in Computer Graphics.

A science degree that combines specialist courses in computer graphics with courses from computer science, media design and engineering, to produce graduates who are immediately employable in games, film, visual effects, and simulation. A design degree, with concentration on 3D Modelling and Animation and Creative Coding. Students may optionally take more technical papers from the computer graphics and computer science programmes.
